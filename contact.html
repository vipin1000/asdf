Frequently Asked Questions

What are your standard commission rates?

Our commission rates vary depending on the property type, location, and services required. For residential properties, our rates typically range from 1.5% to 2.5% of the sale value. Commercial properties are typically 2% to 3%. Please contact our team for a personalized quote.

How long does the property buying process take?

The timeline can vary significantly depending on various factors including property type, location, financing, and legal complexities. On average, the process takes between 45-90 days from offer acceptance to closing. Our experts will provide you with a more accurate timeline based on your specific situation.

What documents do I need to sell my property?

Required documents typically include proof of ownership, identity documents, property tax receipts, sanctioned building plans, completion certificate, NOC from society (if applicable), and encumbrance certificate. Our legal team will guide you through the specific documentation needed for your property.

Do you help with home loans and financing?

Yes, we have partnerships with leading banks and financial institutions to assist our clients with competitive home loan options. Our financial advisors can help you understand the various loan products available and guide you through the application process.
